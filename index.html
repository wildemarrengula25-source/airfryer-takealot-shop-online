Description

Kit out your kitchen in one go with the Russell Hobbs Kitchen Essentials Bundle. It pairs a 1.7L cordless brushed stainless steel kettle, a 4-slice stainless steel toaster and a 20L manual microwave, giving you everything you need for breakfast and quick meals. The kettle boils fast with a 360° cordless base, the toaster offers variable browning across four slices, and the 700W microwave handles reheating, cooking and defrosting with simple rotary controls — all finished in matching brushed stainless steel.

Features:

  • Complete 3-piece set: kettle, 4-slice toaster and microwave.
  • Matching brushed stainless steel finish.
  • 1.7L rapid-boil kettle with 360° cordless base.
  • Removable, washable limescale filter on the kettle.
  • 4-slice toaster with variable browning control.
  • Toaster defrost and reheat functions plus removable crumb tray.
  • 20L microwave with 700W of cooking power.
  • 5 microwave power levels and easy rotary dials.
  • 35-minute microwave timer with defrost setting.
  • Cool-touch handles and non-slip feet throughout.

Specifications:

  • Kettle Capacity: 1.7L.
  • Kettle: 360° cordless base, removable limescale filter.
  • Toaster: 4 slices, variable browning, defrost & reheat.
  • Microwave Capacity: 20L.
  • Microwave Power: 700W.
  • Microwave Power Levels: 5.
  • Microwave Timer: 35 minutes.
  • Material: Brushed Stainless Steel & Plastic.
  • Colour: Stainless Steel / Silver.
  • Brand: Russell Hobbs.
  • Warranty: 12 Months.
